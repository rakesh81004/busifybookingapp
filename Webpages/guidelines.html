1.Follow the step-by-step instructions provided on the booking platform. Double-check the selected route, date, and time before confirming the booking. Choose preferred seats if available and review the total fare, including any additional charges or taxes Use secure payment methods such as credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, or net banking.

2.Avoid sharing financial information via email or messaging platforms. Keep records of transaction details, including confirmation emails and receipts. Save contact information for customer support provided by the booking platform or bus operator. Reach out to customer support for assistance with bookings, cancellations, or any other inquiries.

3.Be patient and provide necessary details when seeking assistance to expedite the resolution process. Share feedback and reviews about your booking experience to help improve services. Provide constructive feedback to highlight both positive experiences and areas for improvement. Consider leaving reviews on the booking platform, social media, or review websites to assist other travelers.

4.Follow safety guidelines and protocols issued by health authorities and transportation authorities. Wear masks, use hand sanitizers, and maintain physical distancing while traveling on buses. Report any safety concerns or violations to the bus staff or relevant authorities promptly.
